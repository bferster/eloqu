eloqú

eloqú is a web-based media player produced by the Middleburg Museum to offer a more interactive and visual way to experience rich oral histories. To make them more accessible, we made this player to make them more interactive and visual, by adding a clickable transcript, where you can can easily do keyword searches, and see images and maps that show locations in context.
  • plays the audio, with synched transcript
  • Drag time bar
    to scrub entire interview
  • Click on red boxes in transcript to view pictures in viewer
  • Click on green boxes to view location on map
  • Click on blue boxes to view web sites
  • Click on word cloud button to see word cloud of transcript at this point
  • Clicking on a word in the cloud searches for that word
